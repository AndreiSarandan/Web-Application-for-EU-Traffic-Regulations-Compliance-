Low Emission Zones in Portugal

 Portugal has a Low Emission Zone (LEZ) in Lisbon, designed to improve air quality by restricting access to vehicles that do not meet at least Euro 3 standards.
 This LEZ operates from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM on weekdays, ensuring that higher-emission vehicles are kept out of the city center during the busiest hours. The goal is to reduce pollution levels and promote a healthier environment for residents and visitors. Vehicles that do not comply with these standards are prohibited from entering the designated areas during these times, contributing to a significant reduction in emissions and traffic congestion.
