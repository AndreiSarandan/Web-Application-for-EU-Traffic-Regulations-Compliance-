Low Emission Zones in United Kingdom

 Low Emission Zones (known as Clean Air Zones) are established in several cities. These zones follow a national LEZ framework and affect different vehicle types. While stickers are not needed, registration is required for foreign vehicles. Generally, if a vehicle complies with the minimum emission standards, no registration is necessary, and this can be verified using the Route Planner.
 However, if a vehicle does not comply with the standards, a daily registration fee can be paid before entering the LEZ.. Some cities have Low Emission Zones that affect only local buses under specific agreements. Additionally, there are several other urban area road tolling schemes.
