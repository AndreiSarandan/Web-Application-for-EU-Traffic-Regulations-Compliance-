Low Emission Zones in Netherlands

 The Netherlands has a national framework for Low Emission Zones (LEZ), known as 'Milieuzone', which exclusively apply to diesel vehicles. Vehicles using other types of fuel are always allowed to enter these zones.
 Access to these zones is regulated based on the Euro standard, differentiating between light-duty vehicles on one hand and heavy-duty vehicles and/or coaches on the other. No stickers or registration are required, as compliance is ensured through cameras and special enforcement officers.
